Method 2: One Way ANOVA Test
The one way analysis of variance (ANOVA) is used to determine statistical differences between means of three or more independent mostly unrelated groups. In one way ANOVA there are two possible hypothesis: One is null hypothesis and another one is the alternative hypothesis. We used One- Way ANOVA test on ethnic group data to check if they are related. We have following hypothesis for One –Way ANOVA test.
H0: (Null Hypothesis) states that there is no significant difference between the groups and equality between means.
H1 :( Alternate Hypothesis) states that there is difference between the means and the groups.
We are considering alpha= 0.05
